Kaz PhD association the United Kingdom

Kazakhstan PhD Association in the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland would be honoured to invite established researchers, PhD and Master students of your university to the Second Central Asian Research Forum on Sustainable Development and Innovation, which will take place in Loughborough (on 18 March 2019 ), United Kingdom, and in Astana (4 April 2019), Kazakhstan. The Second Central Asian Research Forum on Sustainable Development and Innovation provides a platform to attract potential partners from government agencies and business structures to research projects of Kazakhstani young scientists, which are in line with the UN's Sustainable Development Goals. It is hoped that the Forum will result in a synergy between industry and young scientists, and aid the development of Kazakhstan and the other countries of Central Asia. The Forum also seeks to establish partnership links between the universities of Kazakhstan and United Kingdom. Notably, the Forum in the United Kingdom is organised in partnership with Loughborough University, which is ranked in the top 10 UK's universities and has a vast experience in the implementation of scientific projects aimed at sustainable development. The Forum has received a great support from the government, industries, and academic institutions. The Sponsors and Partners of the Forum are as follows: The Administration of the President of Kazakhstan The Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan The National Welfare Fund "Samruk-Kazyna"   The Embassy of Kazakhstan in the United Kingdom The Embassy of the United Kingdom in Kazakhstan Loughborough University British Council The British-Kazakh Society The European Society for Central Asian Studies,  National Company "KAZAKH INVEST" Please, find attached leaflets of the Forum, which contain information for your students on how to apply and deadlines. We would greatly appreciate if the staff of your university could share the information about our Forum with your students.  Kazakhstan PhD Association in the UK is a society of Kazakhstani PhD students studying in the UK. At the moment, we have 72 PhD Candidates, and our research areas include Investment, Business Management, Blockchain, Finance, Economics, Petrochemistry, Agriculture, Polymer Chemistry, Engineering, Power Engineering, Law, Hydrology, Geology and IT.  We are sincerely looking forward to receiving projects from your students and looking forward to seeing them at our Forum. For more information and enquiries, please, contact us via e-mail: kazphdassociationuk@gmail.com
